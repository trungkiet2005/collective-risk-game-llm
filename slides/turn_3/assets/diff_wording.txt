THAY ĐỔI 1 — bỏ lối kể chuyện, nêu thẳng xác suất

  [CŨ]   - If the group does not reach 120: the computer runs a lottery: with
           probability 90% the group is hit by a disaster and every player loses
           all of their remaining money (final cash payoff 0); with the
           remaining 10% probability, each player keeps their remaining money.

  [MỚI]  - If the group does not reach 120, with probability 90% every player in
           the group loses all of their remaining money (final cash payoff 0);
           with the remaining 10% probability, each player keeps their remaining
           money.

  Bỏ đúng hai hình ảnh: "the computer runs a lottery" (máy quay xổ số) và
  "the group is hit by a disaster" (nhóm gặp thảm hoạ). Con số 90% giữ nguyên.
